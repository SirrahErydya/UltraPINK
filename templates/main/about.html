{% load static %} About

UltraPINK allows you to load, store, and inspect data that is analysable with a self-organizing Kohonen map that was created with PINK. You can add multiple SOMs to a project, have them displayed, and let UltraPINK show you the best fits from your data set to each cell of the map.

Futhermore, it is possible to label single data samples or map entries to allow a semi-automatic data analysis. You can export the final catalog of all inspected, and labeled or unlabeled samples to a csv file.

This tool is currently under development and will gain more inspection features, as well as a direct connection to PINK allowing visualized training of SOMs directly with UltraPINK.
